Why We Do What We Do?

At Hello Speech Therapy, we believe everyone has a story worth telling, and everyone has a voice deserves to be heard. By building a team of experienced and motivated therapists, aided with state of the art technology, we strive every day to make quality speech therapy affordable and accessible to people in need. For timid children who try to make friends on the playground, for dreamers who want to get their ideas across, and for those with a life of stories to be shared, we do what we do, because we believe life matters, and everyone deserves the best possible quality of it.

The Team

At Hello Speech Therapy, we believe our greatest strength is our team. We believe it is not only nice to treat every team member with care and respect, it is also the right thing to do as an organization. When a hospital lays off dozens of its employees, it sends a message to its employees that their quality of work does not matter, their care for the patients does not matter, and their employees don't matter. For those who are not laid off, it creates a toxic work environment of constant anxiety, distrust among teammates, and potentially a back-stabbing culture where peopleâ€™s only focus is saving their own jobs. Most people would find it hard to even perform normal tasks in such environment, needless to mention performing intensive medical care. In contrast, Hello Speech Therapy is committed to providing a welcoming, collaborative and safe work environment where everyone feels like home. We are committed to pay $18 per hour minimum wage and never lay off any full-time employee under any circumstance, so everyone can focus on providing the best services possible to our clients. To us, profit is only a byproduct of doing outstanding work, not the final goal of everything we do.

The Innovation

We are an innovative organization that challenges the status quo and strives to innovate new and better approaches to improving the efficiency and quality of care. Not only do we aim at leveraging technology to measure and improve the effectiveness of our treatments, we also strive to create new treatment formats that are accessible and affordable to people in need. We envision a world where essential medical care can be universally accessible, despite the physical location of financial resources. We believe our work in deploying cutting edge technological advancements such as virtual reality, machine learning, cognitive computing will not only benefit the clients we see in clinics but also people around the world.

Final Words

We believe our innovation will make speech therapy accessible and affordable to many more people in need. We believe by treating people over profit, we can build a dynamic team of therapists to provide quality treatments. We believe our hard work will improve the quality of countless lives around us. As a young organization, we hereby humbly invite those who share our visions to guide us, help us, and join us to build a future we all believe in.
